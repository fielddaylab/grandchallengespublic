How Will The Grand Challenges Move Forward?

How Will The Grand Challenges Support Collaboration?

While collaboration is an essential component of creating innovative solutions, it is also hard work. Collaborating can take extra time and brings added stress to any project. The Grand Challenges addresses these issues by connecting faculty to one another and desired community partners to facilitate collaboration. The Network staff will also offer feedback to help refine project ideas.

How Can We Connect Research With Impact?

The Wisconsin Idea, the foundation of the University of Wisconsin system, is based on the notion that research needs to have an impact on the community. Research doesn’t have to end up siloed in the University. It can and should make a difference in the lives of the residents of the state of Wisconsin and beyond. The Grand Challenges were created to connect research with impact.

How Will The Grand Challenges Encourage Cross-Disciplinary Partnerships?

Cross-disciplinary collaboration goes against the grain at the university where faculty are only recognized for individual work and research. For faculty working on a project, bringing in partners requires extra time and effort. Even finding the right partners can be an insurmountable challenge. These hurdles make it difficult for faculty, researchers, and community members to work together. The Grand Challenges team works to make collaboration possible by supporting the work financially at every phase. We will help you find the right partners and then work to facilitate a smooth team partnership by meeting administrative and technical needs.

How Will We Engage Community Partners?

The Grand Challenges will be enriching and productive for both researchers and the community partners involved. To guarantee equal and productive partnerships, The Grand Challenges will offer guidance from the School of Education faculty at every stage of multi-tiered grant funding process, to guarantee that the projects are worthwhile, important, and fair to all parties involved.
